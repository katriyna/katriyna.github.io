About NёrdCamp

For the fifth time NёrdCamp is gathering guests: highly motivated students, young professionals from modern creative spheres: programmers, designers, product managers, aspiring entrepreneurs - and experts, who are willing to share the experience with younger colleagues.

NёrdCamp is being organized by interdisciplinary research and education program GameChangers. There are descriptions of previous meetings: link & link — on the official site.

This year it will be held on April 25-27.

Spring, countryside recreation center, comfortable holiday cottages, training sessions and evening party with BBQ… All this creates unique atmosphere.

It is up to participants how to use it. It is possible to listen about things which are outside your usual specialisation or to exchange experience in your sphere. You can try yourself as an organizer of a master class, a discussion session or a business game. Also NёrdCamp is a good chance to extend your social network with new contacts.

Positive and friendly NёrdCamp’s environment will encourage you to spend time effectively. Will glad to see you soon!

Registration fee

Registration fee is 4000 rubles for students and 5000 rubles for those who are already working. It includes:
  • Accommodation — 2 nights
  • 3 meals a day
  • Renting of a conference room
  • BBQ

Location & Accommodation

NёrdCamp'14 will take place in countryside recreation center Orech, which is located in Leningradskaya oblast', 67th kilometer of highway Priozersky. Participants will be accommodated in countryside quadruple cottages.
